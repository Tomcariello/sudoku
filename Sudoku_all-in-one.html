Gameboard

This tool is designed to enhance your Sudoku experience. By pressing the "Analyze" button, the current game board will be analyzed and the hint area at the bottom of the screen will be updated.

If a position in the hint area is WHITE, that means there is only 1 option left for that item & it is automatically reflected on the gameboard.

If a position has multiple possibilities remaining, it will be highlighted in GREEN. The lighter/brighter the green, the fewer possibilities remain for that position.
