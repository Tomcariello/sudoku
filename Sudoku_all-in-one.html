Gameboard

This tool is designed to enhance your Sudoku experience. By pressing the "Analyze" button, the current game board will be analyzed and the hint area at the bottom of the screen will be updated.

If a position in the hint area is WHITE, that means there is only 1 option left for that item & it is automatically reflected on the gameboard.

If a position has multiple possibilities remaining, it will be highlighted in GREEN. The lighter the green, the fewer possibilities remain for that position.

Gameboard Hints:

How to use the hints to improve your game:

In a game of Sudoku, each box must contain a digit between 1 and 9. When starting a new game, each gameboard box is represented in the hint area with all of the possible options; all digits from 1 to 9.

When number(s) are entered into the gameboard & the Analyze button is pressed, the possible options per box will be assessed and the values in each box will be adjusted.

The hint area will quickly alert the human player to areas of interest (boxes with a low number of possible options remaining) for further advancement in the game.
